a mindful breakfast & lunch café

The Lazy Tulip Café is an independently owned café located on the west side of downtown Barrie. Our specialty is providing breakfast & lunch made with fresh ingredients, locally raised meats and free run eggs. We make everything in house and infuse it with love! Including our bread, baked goods, soup, quiche, baked French toast, all our sauces and berry jam.

Our coffee comes from Planet Bean, a small fair trade/organic micro-roaster located in Guelph, Ontario. Our daily medium roast is the Feminino coffee.

Enjoy sustainably sourced teas from Pluck Tea in Toronto. Ask for our tea menu & loose leaf sample box.

Enjoying a meal at The Lazy Tulip is a culinary journey; our award winning chefs use the freshest ingredients available and cook all items as per order to enhance the taste of all meals.

We hope to see you soon!
